Evolution of Experience

Sunday, March 9th

How we develop has changed just as much as the experience our users have in our products. From scripting and scaffolding enabled by node.js to the new realities of building realtime and responsive applications you'll learn from the experience of industry and community leaders.

Topics: npm, browserify, backbone

Scaling Down and Up

Monday, March 10th

Can the tools we use to build a website in half a day also help us add features to an app that deploys to millions? Learn about tools and tricks to help you start fast and scale big in the front-end to the back-end.

Topics: node.js, WebSockets, WebRTC, bootstrap
