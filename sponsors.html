Party Time!

Every sponsorship puts your brand and your brand only on a party of your choosing.

Target Audience.

The evening parties are broken in to tracks: node.js, front-end, JSBots and mobile. This lets you pick the audience you want to reach most.

Big Exposure.

JSFest brings together a huge audience around a series of events and parties. Your logo, next to the party you choose, will be viewed by all.

Your brand on a party.

Every sponsor picks the day and track they'd like to sponsor the party for. The sponsor rate is a flat fee per party. Party sponsorships, usually reserved for top level sponsors, ensure good brand recognition and ROI.

Reach your audience. Pick your track.

Parties are separated in to tracks and held at various bars in San Francisco. Each track draws a portion of the attendance around their specializations. In picking your track you get to pick what part of the community you'd like to reach.

Big crowd. Big exposure.

JSFest brings together a broad audience of different backgrounds together. While your sponsorship puts your brand on specific track and party your logo is seen on the site and visible by every attendee.

Pick a party.

Choose from any free part slot in any track. The sponsorship price is per party and the price is fixed. There is no discount available for sponsoring multiple parties because the price is tied closely to the cost of each party.

Open Bar

Your sponsored party will have an open bar up to $5K.

Swag and Banners

You can set up any banners you'd like at your party and hand out whatever swag you like. Because of the distributed nature of the other conference events there are no banners or swag available at event registration or during any talks. Some venues may have limitations one what you can hand, particularly if you intend to hand out food or drinks. Please inform us ahead of time if you intend to hand out another other than the standard swag.

Bring your team

You can bring as many of people from your own company as you would like to your party. Sponsorship does not include tickets to other conference events or parties other than the ones you sponsor.

Code of Conduct

JSFest is dedicated to a harassment-free experience. We expect all sponsors and their employees to follow our code of conduct at parties and appreciate your help upholding this code of conduct during your party.
